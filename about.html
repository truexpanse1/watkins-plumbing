Our Story

A Legacy of Quality Plumbing

Watkins Plumbing has been a trusted name in Northern California plumbing for over 25 years. When Jerrod Loewen purchased the company, he made a commitment to keep the Watkins Plumbing name — and the reputation for honest, reliable work that comes with it.

Under Jerrod's ownership, Watkins Plumbing continues to serve homeowners and businesses across six Northern California counties with the same dedication to quality craftsmanship, upfront pricing, and genuine customer care that the company was built on.

Whether it's a late-night emergency, a complete sewer line replacement, or a simple faucet repair — our team treats every job like it's in our own home. That's the Watkins Plumbing promise.
