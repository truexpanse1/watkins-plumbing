Energy Efficient Tankless Water Heater Installation in Chico, CA

Tankless water heaters are transforming the way Chico homeowners think about hot water. When comparing tankless vs tank water heater options, the differences are clear: unlike traditional tank-style heaters that store and continuously reheat 40-80 gallons of water, tankless units heat water instantly as it flows through the device — giving you an endless supply of hot water on demand.

The benefits are significant: energy savings of 24-34% compared to conventional tanks, a compact wall-mounted design that frees up valuable floor space, and a lifespan of 20+ years (nearly double that of a traditional unit). As an energy efficient water heater option, a whole house tankless water heater is one of the smartest investments you can make. You may also qualify for a tankless water heater rebate in California, further reducing the overall tankless water heater cost.

At Watkins Plumbing, we service and install all major brands including Rinnai tankless, Navien tankless, Noritz, Rheem, and Takagi models. Whether you need tankless water heater repair on an existing unit or a brand new installation, we handle both new installs and retrofits, working with gas and electric models to find the perfect fit for your home's hot water demands and budget.

Our team will assess your household's hot water usage, recommend the right size unit, handle all necessary gas line or electrical upgrades, and ensure everything is up to code. We also provide tankless water heater maintenance including annual flushing to keep your system running at peak efficiency. We stand behind every installation with our 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Signs You Should Switch to an On Demand Water Heater

  • You're constantly running out of hot water during showers or back-to-back usage
  • Your energy bills are higher than they should be from heating water around the clock
  • Your current water heater is over 10 years old and nearing end of life
  • You want to go green and reduce your home's energy consumption
  • You need more space — your bulky tank is taking up valuable room
  • You're building a new home and want the most efficient option from the start

A tankless water heater typically costs more upfront than a traditional tank — usually $2,500 to $4,500 installed versus $1,200 to $2,500 for a standard tank. However, tankless units last 20+ years (versus 8-12 for tanks) and save 24-34% on energy costs. Over the lifetime of the unit, most homeowners come out significantly ahead with tankless.
The U.S. Department of Energy estimates tankless water heaters are 24-34% more energy efficient than conventional storage tanks for homes that use 41 gallons or less of hot water daily. For homes using more, savings are around 8-14%. That translates to $100 or more per year in energy savings for most Chico households.
