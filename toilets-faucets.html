Toilet, Faucet & Sink Repair in Chico, CA

Toilets, faucets, and sinks are the most-used fixtures in your home — and when they break down, it disrupts your entire day. Whether it's a toilet that won't stop running, a faucet that drips all night, or a sink that drains too slowly, Watkins Plumbing has the experience and parts to fix it fast.

We handle toilet repair and installation including fill valve replacement, flapper repair, wax ring seals, flush mechanisms, and complete toilet replacements. For faucets, we service and install all major brands including Delta, Moen, Kohler, and Pfister — from standard repairs to upgrades like touchless and pull-down models.

Our services also cover garbage disposal repair and installation, shower valve replacement, sink drain repair, and all related fixture work. We carry common parts on our trucks so most repairs are completed in a single visit with no waiting for special orders.

A running toilet can waste over 200 gallons of water per day. A dripping faucet wastes thousands of gallons per year. These aren't just annoyances — they're costing you real money on your water bill. Let Watkins fix it today.

Signs You Need Fixture Repair

  • Running toilet that won't stop — this wastes up to 200 gallons of water per day
  • Dripping faucet that keeps you up at night and inflates your water bill
  • Slow sink drain that takes forever to empty
  • Wobbly toilet that rocks when you sit down — a sign of wax ring failure
  • Low water pressure at specific fixtures (faucets or showerheads)
  • Garbage disposal that's jammed, humming but not working, or leaking

Our Repair Process

  1. Diagnose the Issue: Our technician inspects the fixture, identifies the root cause of the problem, and explains exactly what's going on in plain language.
  2. Recommend Repair or Upgrade: We tell you honestly whether the fixture can be repaired cost-effectively or if replacement makes more sense for your budget and long-term value.
  3. Upfront Price: You get a clear, written price before we touch a single tool. No hourly surprises, no hidden add-ons.
  4. Complete the Work: We perform the repair or installation using quality parts, ensuring everything functions perfectly and meets code.
  5. Test Everything: We run water, flush toilets, check for leaks, and verify full function before we consider the job done.
  6. Clean Up: We leave your bathroom or kitchen cleaner than we found it. No mess, no debris, no trace we were there except a perfectly working fixture.

The average garbage disposal lasts 8 to 15 years depending on usage and maintenance. Signs it's time for replacement include frequent jams, persistent odors despite cleaning, leaking from the bottom, and the motor humming but not grinding. We can repair most disposals, but if yours is at the end of its life, we'll recommend an upgrade and install it the same day.
