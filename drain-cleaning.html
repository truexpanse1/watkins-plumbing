Signs You Need Drain Cleaning

  • Slow drains — Water pools in sinks, tubs, or showers and takes a long time to drain
  • Gurgling sounds — You hear bubbling or gurgling noises coming from drains or toilets
  • Bad odors — Foul, sewage-like smells are coming from your drains
  • Multiple clogged drains — More than one fixture is draining slowly at the same time
  • Water backing up — Water backs up in the tub when you flush, or in the sink when the dishwasher runs
  • Fruit flies — Small flies appearing near drains indicate organic buildup inside the pipes

Our Drain Cleaning Process

  1. Inspection: We start with a thorough inspection of the affected drains, and if needed, use a fiber optic camera to see exactly what's causing the blockage inside the pipe.
  2. Diagnosis: Once we know what we're dealing with — grease, hair, soap scum, tree roots, or a collapsed pipe — we explain the problem and give you an upfront quote before any work begins.
  3. Clearing Method: We select the best method for your specific clog. This could be professional snaking, hydro jetting for tougher blockages, or a combination of techniques to fully clear the line.
  4. Test Flow: After clearing the clog, we run water through the system to verify full flow is restored and check for any secondary issues.
  5. Cleanup: We clean up our work area, share maintenance recommendations to prevent future clogs, and make sure you're 100% satisfied before we leave.
